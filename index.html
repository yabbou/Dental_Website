Services

Implant Dentures

Denture implants help preserve the bone and maintain the natural facial structure by immitating the natural tooth root system.

Full & Partial Detures

Full dentures are for patients who are missing all of their teeth, either on the top or bottom arch. Partial dentures are for patients missing some teeth, with metal clasps holding on to the remaining natural teeth.

Denture Repairs

Dentures may crack or chip even with regualr use, or perhaps not fit as securely as when you first bought them, since your gums and jaw bone shrink overtime. This discomfort can be addressed with a denture repair.

Flexible Scheduling

Whether an emergeny or just a casual check-up, a weekend or in the evening, we are always here to help.

Toronto & GTA-Wide
Servicing

Our denturist will travel far and wide to attend to your denture needs.

Rebase and Reline

To refit the acrylic inner surface of your denture to your gums, relining would be necessary. When the acyrlic material needs a complete replacing, the denturist will retain the original denture teeth and gladly rebase your dentures.

Our Mobile Services

House call appointments are our specialty. Since we understand that patients may not always be in the position to come to our clinic, we make it a priority to bring the clinic to them!
